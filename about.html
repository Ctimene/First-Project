Behind The Name

This page isn't just about me, but the website's namesake, Citmene.

Ctimene is the daughter of Laertes and Anticlea, the former King and Queen of Ithaca, and the younger sister of Odysseus, the current King of Ithaca. Ctimene is married to Eurylochus, who is the second-in-command of Odysseus' ship during the return to Ithaca after the Trojan War. Both Ctimene and her husband were from the island Same, near Ithaca.
She is a character from Homer's epic poem, the Odyssey, which tells the story of Odysseus's journey home after the Trojan War.
